Revamp and Documentation of a Private Institute's Website

Internship/Junior (Apr 2023 - Oct 2023)

The project involved a comprehensive overhaul of a private institute's website, beginning with a thorough review of the existing site. The redesign process included building the website from the ground up, utilizing data from a database to ensure accuracy and relevance. One of the key objectives was to streamline the registration process by implementing automation mechanisms, facilitating seamless data storage in the database.

The project relied on WordPress as the primary platform, supplemented by custom plugins tailored to meet the client's specific requirements. The focus was on achieving a complete website transformation while retaining the core structure and functionality. Additionally, the project entailed the integration of new features and backend automations to enhance operational efficiency.

Please note that the availability of the site may vary due to inconsistent management or a lack of ongoing maintenance by the institution.
